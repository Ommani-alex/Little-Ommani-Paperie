Shop & shipping FAQ

What are the sticker sheets made from?

Most sheets are printed on quality vinyl sticker paper and kiss-cut for easy peeling. Product listings will always include the exact material and size.

How quickly do orders ship?

For a small handmade shop, allow 3 to 7 business days for processing before shipping. Seasonal drops may take a little longer.

Can I send an order as a gift?

Yes. Add a gift note at checkout and keep an eye out for future gift-wrap and happy-mail add-ons.
