Shop & shipping FAQ

What are the sticker sheets made from?

Each listing states its exact material. The current sheets are printed on vinyl sticker paper and kiss-cut for easy peeling.

These current sticker sheets are not waterproof. They are best for journals, planners, paper crafts, and other indoor uses.
