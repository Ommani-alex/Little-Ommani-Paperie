1. Replace `YOUR_PUBLIC_TEST_API_KEY_HERE` in `index.html`, `ghostly-grind.html`, and `pretty-in-pink-coffee-run.html` with your live Snipcart public API key.
2. Connect the newsletter form to an email provider such as Buttondown, MailerLite, Flodesk, or Shopify Email. It is currently a visual placeholder and does not store email addresses.
