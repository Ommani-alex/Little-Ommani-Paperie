Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Little Ommani Paperie ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect when you visit littleommani.com or place an order, and how it's used.

Information we collect

  • Order information: When you make a purchase, our checkout provider (Snipcart, with Stripe as the payment processor) collects your name, email address, shipping address, and payment details. We never see or store your full payment card number ourselves — that's handled directly by Stripe.
  • Email list: If you choose to join our email list, we collect your email address through MailerLite, our email service provider.
  • Basic site usage: Like most websites, our hosting and service providers may collect standard technical information such as browser type and pages visited.

How we use your information

  • To process and ship your order
  • To respond to questions or order issues
  • To send you shop updates, if you've opted in to our email list

We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

Third-party services

We work with a small number of trusted services to run this shop:

  • Snipcart — shopping cart and checkout
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • MailerLite — email list management
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

Cookies

Our checkout and email signup tools may use cookies or similar technology to function correctly — for example, to keep items in your cart. We don't use cookies for advertising.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from our email list at any time using the link in any email we send. For questions about your order information, contact us directly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

Questions

Email littleommani@gmail.com with any privacy questions.
