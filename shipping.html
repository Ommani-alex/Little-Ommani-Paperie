Shipping

$5.99 flat-rate U.S. shipping. Free U.S. shipping on orders $35 and up.

Processing time

Please allow 3–7 business days for your order to be carefully prepared before it ships. Weekends and holidays do not count as business days. During seasonal drops or higher-volume periods, processing may take a little longer.

Where we ship

Little Ommani Paperie currently ships within the United States. International shipping is not available at this time.

Delayed or missing packages

Once an order has been handed to the carrier, delivery timing is outside of our control. If tracking hasn't updated for several business days or your package appears to be missing, contact us and we'll help you review the next steps.
